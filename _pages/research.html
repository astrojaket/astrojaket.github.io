Gas Giant Exoplanets

Research on gas giant exoplanets focuses on the atmospheric properties and composition of these large planets outside our solar system. This includes studies of their weather systems, cloud formations, and potential habitability.

My work on terrestrial exoplanets investigates rocky planets in habitable zones of other stars, including their atmospheric characteristics, surface conditions, and prospects for life.
